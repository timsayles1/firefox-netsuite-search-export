NetSuite Field Finder

Export your saved searches to Suitescript.

For NetSuite developers who want to export saved searches for use in their code.

Supports SS1.0 and SS2.X.

From the "Edit" mode of a Saved Search in NetSuite, click the "Export as SuiteScript".

Please note that your search must be saved before it can be exported.

**Some search types are not supported.

You must save the search before attempting to export.
